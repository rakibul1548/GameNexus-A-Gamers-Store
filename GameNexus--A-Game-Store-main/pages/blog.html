Ancient Tech Weapon Locations

Egypt and World Space Wars
PharaohHunter
Posted on June 15, 2023
Discover all hidden ancient Egyptian tech weapons across the solar system. The Plasma Staff of Ra is particularly powerful against the Martian forces. Look for hidden chambers in the Great Pyramid level. Here's a detailed breakdown of all weapon locations: 1. Plasma Staff of Ra - Found in the hidden chamber beneath the Sphinx. You'll need to solve the constellation puzzle on the wall first. 2. Anubis Blade - Located in the Mars pyramid complex. Defeat the guardian boss in the central chamber to claim this weapon. 3. Horus Shield - Hidden in the asteroid belt level. Look for a glowing rock formation near waypoint Delta-7. Each weapon has unique abilities: - Plasma Staff: Charged shot that penetrates multiple enemies - Anubis Blade: Life-steal effect on hit - Horus Shield: Creates a temporary energy barrier Pro Tip: Combine the Plasma Staff with the Osiris armor set for a 30% damage boost against mechanical enemies.
